Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the rafting site is to establish an online presence for our rafting business, showcasing our services, engaging with potential customers, and providing an informative and user-friendly experience.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure enthusiasts, families looking for outdoor activities, and corporate groups seeking team-building experiences.

Scenario Questions:

  • Who is looking for a thrilling outdoor activity for the weekend? - Adventure enthusiasts.
  • Which group is searching for a fun, family-friendly experience? - Families with children.
  • What type of client seeks a unique team-building event? - Corporate groups.

Typography

Headings Example Font

The font chosen for headings is 'Roboto', a sans-serif font that is modern and easy to read.

Normal Text/Paragraph Example Font

The font chosen for normal text is 'Open Sans', a sans-serif font that is clean and versatile.

Colored Callout Example Font

The font for callouts is 'Montserrat', a sans-serif font that is bold and eye-catching.
